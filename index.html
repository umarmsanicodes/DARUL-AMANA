About Us

Nurturing Huffaz Since Day One

DARUL-AMANA WAL IHSAN is a premier full-time Guardians school dedicated to helping children from any country memorize the Holy Quran in a structured, safe, and spiritually enriching environment.

We welcome students aged 10 to 20 into our boys' and girls' sections, providing 24-hour supervision, three daily meals, comfortable dormitories, and a rigorous 3+ year Hifz program guided by qualified teachers.
