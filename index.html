About Us

Your Child's Trusted Home in Egypt

DARUL-AMANA WAL IHSAN is a trusted International Student Guardianship Center based in Egypt, providing comprehensive care and support for students from Nigeria and around the world. We offer safe accommodation, daily supervision, student welfare, and guidance in a secure Islamic environment.

We also assist families with admission into Primary Schools, Preparatory Schools, Secondary Schools, Universities, and other educational centers according to the parents' choice. In addition, we provide Qur'an Memorization, Tajweed, Arabic Language, and Islamic Studies under qualified instructors.

Our mission is to ensure that every student receives quality education, proper care, and a successful academic journey while living under our guardianship in Egypt.

📋 Policy

Admission Requirements & Guardianship Policy

Before accepting any student under the care of DARUL-AMANA WAL IHSAN, parents or guardians are kindly requested to meet the following requirements:

01

Valid Identification

The student must have a valid passport and all required travel documents for entry into Egypt.

02

Parent/Guardian Consent

A parent or legal guardian must complete the registration process and agree to our guardianship policies.

03

School Admission

Parents may choose any recognized school or institution in Egypt, and we will assist with the admission process according to their preference.

04

Student Conduct

Students are expected to maintain good Islamic manners, respect teachers, staff, fellow students, and follow the rules of the guardianship center.

05

Health Information

Parents must provide accurate medical information, including allergies, medications, or any health conditions that require special attention.

06

Fees & Expenses

All agreed guardianship, accommodation, and educational service fees must be paid according to the agreed schedule.

07

Communication

Parents should maintain regular communication with the guardianship center and provide updated contact information at all times.
